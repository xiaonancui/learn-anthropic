## One-Liner

Investigates induction heads in transformers—a mechanism enabling in-context learning by copying and completing patterns from previous context.

## Key Points

- Induction heads are transformer attention heads that enable in-context learning
- They work by copying and completing patterns from previous context
- Key mechanism for how transformers learn to generalize from few examples
