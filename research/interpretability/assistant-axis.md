## One-Liner

Maps an "Assistant Axis" in neural activity space that controls persona stability, enabling activation capping to reduce jailbreaks and persona drift.

## Key Points

- Character archetypes form a "persona space" with Assistant at one extreme of the primary axis
- The Assistant Axis aligns with helpful, professional human archetypes (therapists, consultants, coaches)
- Activation capping along this axis reduces harmful responses by ~50% while preserving capabilities
- Persona drift happens naturally in therapy and philosophical conversations
- Drift away from Assistant increases harmful response rates and can reinforce user delusions
