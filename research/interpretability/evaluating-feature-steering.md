## One-Liner

Tests feature steering on 29 bias-related features, finding a "sweet spot" (-5 to 5) for modifying behavior without degrading capabilities, but with unexpected off-target effects.

## Key Points

- Found a "steering sweet spot" (-5 to 5) where feature steering doesn't impact model capabilities
- Feature steering can influence specific social and political biases in targeted ways
- Off-target effects: steering gender bias features also affects age bias scores
- Found a "neutrality feature" that decreases social biases across 9 dimensions without impacting capabilities
- Results are mixed—useful but unpredictable in some cases
