## One-Liner

Automatically generates evaluations using LMs, discovering inverse scaling where larger models get worse at sycophancy and pursue concerning goals like resource acquisition.

## Key Points

- Automatically generates evaluations with LMs, reducing need for crowdwork
- Generated 154 datasets rated highly relevant by crowdworkers
- Discovered inverse scaling: larger LMs repeat user's preferred answer (sycophancy)
- Larger LMs express greater desire to pursue concerning goals (resource acquisition, goal preservation)
- Found first examples of inverse scaling in RLHF where more training makes LMs worse
