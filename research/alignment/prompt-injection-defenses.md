## One-Liner

Claude Opus 4.5 achieves major improvements in prompt injection robustness for browser use, reducing attack success rate to 1% through training, classifiers, and red teaming.

## Key Points

- Prompt injections are adversarial instructions hidden in content that AI models process
- Browser use amplifies risk: vast attack surface and many exploitable actions
- Claude Opus 4.5 demonstrates stronger robustness than previous models
- Defenses include RL training to resist injection, improved classifiers, and human red teaming
- 1% attack success rate is significant improvement but problem is not solved
